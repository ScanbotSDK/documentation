list points
static

The coordinates in pixel space of the four corners of the detected barcode.

The coordinates are returned in the following order:

  • 0 : top-left-x,
  • 1 : top-left-y,
  • 2 : top-right-x,
  • 3 : top-right-y,
  • 4 : bottom-right-x,
  • 5 : bottom-right-y,
  • 6 : bottom-left-x,
  • 7 : bottom-left-y
